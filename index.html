📈 Trend

Applications to engineering schools have grown +23% over the last 5 years, driven by demand for digital and energy profiles.

🌍 International

France is the 3rd most popular destination in Europe for international engineering students, after the UK and Germany.

💼 Employment

95% of engineering graduates find employment within 6 months. The median time to first job is 47 days.

Top Grandes Écoles accept fewer than 1 in 7 applicants. CPGE preparation is the standard path.

About French Engineering

A world-class tradition of excellence

France boasts one of the most rigorous and prestigious engineering education systems in the world. The Grandes Écoles d'Ingénieurs have been shaping technical leaders for over two centuries — from aerospace to AI, energy to civil infrastructure.

Whether you pursue a Classe Préparatoire path or enter through an INSA or university IUT, French engineering degrees are recognised globally and open doors across every sector.

🏛️

Grandes Écoles System

Selective institutions offering a 5-year engineering diploma recognised at Master's level.

📐

CPGE — Prépa

Two intense years of maths & sciences before competitive entrance exams (concours).

🌍

International Recognition

CTI-accredited diplomas are the French equivalent of a European engineering Masters.

🔬

Research & Industry

Strong ties with labs like CNRS, CEA and companies like Airbus, Total, and Capgemini.
